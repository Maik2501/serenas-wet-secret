Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 4, 2026

Introduction

Tear Track Journal ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your personal information.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. All journal entries, emotional tracking data, and app preferences are stored locally on your device only. We have no access to your data.

Data Storage

All data is stored exclusively on your device using secure local storage. Your journal entries never leave your device and are not transmitted to any servers.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat: For processing in-app purchases (tips/donations). RevenueCat may collect anonymous purchase data. See their privacy policy.
  • Apple Push Notifications: For optional reminder notifications. No personal data is shared.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your emotional journal data remains private and on your device.

Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it. You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the app's support page on GitHub.
